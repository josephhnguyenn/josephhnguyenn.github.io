ABOUT ME

My Career:

Hi! This is a small website that I put together to aggregate all of my projects, contacts, and work! I feel as if a resume doesn't truly highlight the behind-the-scenes work, not to mention putting a face to the person.

Currently, I am in the process of pursuing my passion in Software Engineering. I took the scenic route by first studying Computer Engineering and then starting my post-grad career with Systems Engineering.

2018 was my first experience within the professional setting of the engineering field. I started as a Defense Contractor with American Technical Services where I was groomed to be a Systems Engineer.

Less than a year later, one of peers decided to bring me aboard to be an official civilian employee for the DoD Navy. In this new position, I was able to do small software engineering tasks such as scripting and be a leader in a modernization project.

Fast forward to now, I am doing Systems Engineering, where I am able to design and facilitate new functionalities for Cubic Transportation Systems.

However, it is with these experiences that I am certain how the engineering process flows and exactly what step of the process I belong in.
